## 4.Use

- edit UML in a DOM elem with **id or class named 'UML'**

- such as

```html
<div class="UML">
    Title: UML Test
    A->B: Normal line
    B-->C: Dashed line
    C->>D: Open arrow
    D-->>A: Dashed open arrow
</div>
```
